Greetings in German üëã

Germans use different greetings depending on the time of day. Let's learn them!

Guten Morgen
Good morning
Pronunciation: GOO-ten MOR-gen
Guten Tag
Good day / Hello
Pronunciation: GOO-ten TAHK
Guten Abend
Good evening
Pronunciation: GOO-ten AH-bent
Hallo!
Hello! (informal, anytime)
Pronunciation: HAH-lo

üé≠ Cultural Insight

In Germany, it's polite to greet people when entering a room or store. Germans shake hands more often than Americans, even with friends. Direct eye contact during greetings is considered respectful!
